Pizza Recipe

Ingredidents

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. First Step: Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C), and lightly grease a pizza pan.
  2. Second Step: Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
  3. Third Step: Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth. You can do this by hand or use a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook to make it easier.
  4. Fourth Step: Let rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Fifth Step: Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.
  6. Sixth Step: Transfer to the prepared pizza pan.
  7. Seventh Step: Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice.
  8. Eighth Step: Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
